Contact FlexTrax

While FlexTrax is an ideal solution for personal trainers and does work, it is not a fully functioning product. It was built at DevMountain as part of a project. As such you are free to use FlexTrax, but no support is currently provided. If you are interested in the technology or design feel free to contact one of the authors.
