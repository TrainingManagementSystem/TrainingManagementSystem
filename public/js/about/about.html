ABOUT

Finally, an app that gives you the freedom and flexibility you need to help your clients meet their fitness goals. That's because was built with personal trainers in mind. Our system is perfect for personal trainers who work independently, but want the ease and convience of a professional fitness tracking system.

TRAINERS

  • Work with clients at any gym from any device

  • Easily track client's progress using dozen of metrics

  • Send clients feedback on their goals

  • Enroll new clients with the touch of a button

  • View your schedule at a glance

CLIENTS

  • Seemlessly update activity and diet using FitBit

  • See daily fitness goals

  • Update profile and goals using one app

  • Motivate to reach new fitness levels
